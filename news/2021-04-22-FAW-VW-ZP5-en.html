FAW-Volkswagen ZP5 Association "Welding Action"

22 Apr 2021

On April 22, 2021, the “Welding Action” seminar organized by FAW-Volkswagen ZP5 Association was successfully held at Chengdu Shirun Plant. The theme of the seminar was “Preventing Missing Welding/Anti-rust” at 9:00 AM, FAW-Volkswagen Changchun Plant Quality Assurance-Manager Luo Xiufeng, ZP5 Association-Chairman Feng Jingjin, Chengdu Factory-Quality Assurance Outsourcing Division Director Hou Zhi, Qingdao Factory Director Shi Xinmin and other 12 ZP5 leaders and experts visited Shirun Factory.

The experts first listened to the company’s introduction by Mr. Yang Yong, the operation manager of Chengdu Shirun, including the company’s organizational planning, development history, business strategy, engineering capabilities, quality control, etc., so that the experts had an intuitive understanding of the company. understanding. Subsequently, Mr. Yang Yong reported to the experts Chengdu Shirun’s special report on “error prevention and rust prevention”, and introduced in detail Chengdu Shirun’s product development, process design, tooling and fixture design, process quality control, staff training, etc. All aspects of the work, and the quality performance achieved since the launch of the “welding operation”. During the reporting process, FAW-Volkswagen leaders and experts were full of interest and active discussions, and the atmosphere on the scene was strong, making the original 30-minute report meeting last for one and a half hours.

After the report, Mr. Zhong Guangzhao, deputy general manager of the components division of Yanlong Group, led the customer to visit Shirun’s production workshop and introduced the company’s laboratory equipment, experimental capabilities, production equipment, and production facilities to FAW-Volkswagen leaders and experts in detail. Process, process quality control, etc. In the welding area, leaders and experts highly praised Shirun’s welding equipment and fixture design concepts. Manager Luo Xiufeng proposed on the spot to name the reference surface wear groove in the Shirun tooling design concept “Shirun wear groove” in FAW Every factory and supplier of Volkswagen promoted the use, and the leaders and experts expressed their approval on the spot.

After visiting the workshop, the leaders and experts returned to the meeting room again, conducted in-depth discussions on issues of mutual concern, and reached a broad consensus. The entire seminar proceeded smoothly. The discussion in the conference room and the on-site visit were very strong. The quality performance and control capabilities of Chengdu Shirun have been unanimously recognized by the leaders and experts of FAW Volkswagen.

Chengdu Shirun will continue to uphold the corporate values ​​of “Integrity, Fairness, Professionalism, Responsibility, and Inclusiveness” and the quality values ​​of “Customer-centered, quality-oriented core competitiveness, pragmatic, innovative and sustainable development”, make persistent efforts, continue to innovate, and create professionalism. The thermoforming production-oriented enterprise provides customers with high-quality products and services.
